About Me

Hello, I'm an enthusiastic programmer, video editor, gamer, and musician based within Coventry, UK.

I graduated with a first in Computer Games Programming (BSc) at DMU in 2020.
In 2021, I acomplished a dream of being an esports competitor, playing Apex Legends semi-professionally as an in-game leader for TENSTAR.
I then became a Games Development and Games Art tutor for college students.

I play games competitively and enjoy exploring the creative aspects of video editing and creating audio-visual art; blending the two hobbies together, by creating fragmovies.

I am always looking for ways to blend my interests in games, editing, and music to create truly unique and compelling pieces of art.
My goal is to continue exploring and experimenting with technology and art, and I believe that my skills and experience let me create visually stunning and immersive experiences.

Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio website, and I hope you enjoy exploring my work!
