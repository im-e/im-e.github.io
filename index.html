notable projects

bloodthirst - c#, unity (ludum dare 51 game jam entry)

bloodthirst is an old school fps doom-like game that was created entirely on my own in 48 hours for the ludum dare 51 game jam based upon the theme 'every 10 seconds'. every asset and piece of code was made entirely from scratch and by myself within those 48 hours. it was ranked in the top 300 of 2450 entries, and ranked in the top 100 for 'mood'.

in bloodthirst, you must reach the end of the level before succumbing to blood loss which takes 10 seconds, killing an enemy will refill your supply reset the timer. your health, and ammo also depend on your blood supply, so every action is critical, and every second is too. a missed shot, or bullet taken can be the factor between death or success.

it has an advanced first person character controller and movement system, shooting and melee mechanic, ai enemies with projectile bullets, 'fuel and life' blood system, introduction and game end screen, level summary screen and shader effects.
you can play it in your browser on my itch page: https://nearnoah.itch.io/bloodthirst


rave Beat - c#, unity

rave Beat is a rhythm game with acessiblity focused design, that still challenges and trains the rhythmical ability of the player.
it was developed independently by myself for my final year project in my third year of university.

i conducted resarch and wrote a paper alongside the design process and development of the game, i looked at how rhythm games can improve ones rhythmical skill, and how rhythmical skills can improve ones health and learning abilities in the real world. my paper also looked into the design of rhythm games, critiquing the downsides they had in terms of accessibility and how many have unfocused design around rhythm timing. i used my resarch to design rave beat to be playable to all, while ensuring the gameplay was focused around rhythmical ability.

it has a precise timing and real-time feedback system The game is fully implemented and functional with a main menu, in-game UI, ranking and records system, alternate gameplay mode, and saving. you can find the code, and my paper on the github repo: https://github.com/im-e/rave-beat
